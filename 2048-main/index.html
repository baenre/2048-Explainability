How to play:

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one! Your goal is ro reach the 2048 tile.

Each turn, you'll be shown a suggested move. You can follow it or play your own move. If follow the suggestion, it counts as agreement. If you ignore it, it counts as disagreement.

If your move and your actual opinion don't match, click [E] to agree or [R] to disagree with the suggestion.

If you are unsure about a given move suggestion, click on [Show explanation] to see the reasoning behind the suggestion. To get additional context for that explanation, click [Show more]. If you deem the explanation as unclear, click [Explanation unhelpful] to mark it as unclear. You can do this for each of the three explanation slides that are generated per turn.

Select a policy by choosing one in the dropdown menu. Play each policy three times until you reach the end of a game. You are welcome to play more, if you want to. Once you are done, please click [Download] and send the file to juwa@uni-bremen.de.

Thank you and have fun!
